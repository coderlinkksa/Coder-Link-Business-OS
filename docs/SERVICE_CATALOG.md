## Recommended Sales Order

The following order is recommended for introducing services to new customers. The goal is to establish a foundation first, then layer on recurring and growth services.

1. **Website Development** — Establish the customer's digital presence. This is the most common entry point and unlocks the highest number of cross-sells.
2. **Website Hosting + Domain Registration + Business Email** — Attach immediately to every website project as non-negotiable recurring services.
3. **Google Business Profile** — Quick win that increases local visibility and builds early trust.
4. **SEO** — Begin building organic traffic from the first month.
5. **Social Media Management + Content Creation** — Grow the brand and keep the customer engaged in an ongoing relationship.
6. **Technical Support Contract** — Introduce early for any customer with IT infrastructure needs; builds recurring revenue and reduces churn.
7. **POS / CRM / ERP / HR System** — Introduce after trust is established, matched to the customer's business type and operational maturity.
8. **Email Marketing + WhatsApp Automation** — Layer on once the customer has a contact base worth communicating with.
9. **Network Installation / CCTV / Access Control / Attendance Systems** — Introduce when a customer has physical premises needs or is growing their team.
10. **Cloud Migration + Server Management** — Introduce to customers who are ready to modernise their infrastructure.
11. **AI Automation** — Introduce when the customer has active processes running and is ready to automate for scale.
12. **Upsell and Renewal** — At every renewal touchpoint, review the customer's current services and introduce the next logical upgrade.
