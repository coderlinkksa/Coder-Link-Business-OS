# Coder Link Business Model

## Company Overview

Coder Link is a Saudi-based technology company providing digital transformation services for small and medium businesses.

The company currently focuses on the Saudi market with a future expansion plan across the GCC.

Coder Link operates as a remote-first company internally while maintaining a professional local presence for customers.

---

## Target Market

Primary Target:

* Small Businesses
* Medium Businesses

Primary Region:

* Saudi Arabia

Future Expansion:

* GCC Countries

## Revenue Model

The company generates revenue through:

* One-time projects
* Annual subscriptions
* Maintenance contracts
* Website hosting renewals
* Custom software development
* Ready-made software sales

Hosting renewals generally begin from the second year.

---

## Business Philosophy

Focus on delivering complete business solutions instead of individual services.

Build long-term customer relationships through recurring services including maintenance, hosting, marketing and continuous improvements.

---

## Strategic Objective

The first objective is to build a repeatable business system capable of attracting, managing and retaining customers while reducing manual work through automation.

---

## Core Business Rule

Business Before Technology.

Technology exists only to improve business results, customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
