# Lead Capture Workflow Specification

## Purpose

Describe the business purpose of capturing leads from the website and other future channels.

## Business Goal

Record every lead.
Prevent lead loss.
Prepare the lead for qualification.

## Input Sources

* Website Contact Form
* Request a Quote Form

## Required Information

* Company Name
* Contact Name
* Phone
* Email
* Service Requested
* Message
* Source
* Submission Date

## Expected Result

A validated lead is ready to enter the CRM pipeline.

## Out of Scope

* Qualification
* Proposal Generation
* Follow-up
* WhatsApp
* Email
* AI
* Automation Logic

## Acceptance Criteria

The specification is clear enough that an implementation can be created later without changing the business requirements.
